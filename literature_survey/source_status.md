# Source Status Notes

The supplement separates source types.

## Research papers / preprints

These are appropriate for formal citation in the paper or journal version: FunSearch, AlphaEvolve-related papers, theorem-proving surveys, autoformalization surveys, AI4Math survey, Barber's paper, Graphic TSP references, and graph theory references.

## Blog posts and talks

Blog posts and talks were useful for shaping the research framing, especially around Aletheia/Gemini, verification, prompt transparency, and human depth versus AI breadth. They should be used carefully. In a formal paper, they are better treated as contextual motivation unless they are backed by a paper or technical report.

## Raw AI outputs

Raw AI outputs are not authoritative sources. They can be studied as data, but every mathematical or bibliographic claim from them must be checked elsewhere.
